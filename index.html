About Us

An automated parking lot system revolutionizes traditional parking by leveraging advanced technology to optimize space, enhance efficiency, and improve user experience.

Objectives

  • Reduced Over Parking: Can control how many cars can park in a parking lot, to avoid too much cars.

  • Reduced Search-time: Real-time information on available space, eliminates the needs for drivers to roam the lot, saving time and reduce frustration.

  • Optimized Space Utilization: Sensors can accurately tracked parked vehicles and maximizing available space and potentially allowing for smaller lot footprints.

  • Reduced Fuel Consumption: By minimizing search time, automated systems can contribute to lower emmisions and fuel consumptions.
